Pamoja Africa Projects and Programs

We work in a wide range of projects located all around Africa. When you go to work with Pamoja Africa, you specify when you want to go, where and what project you want to do. Everywhere our volunteers go, we employ full-time, professional staff to help you be effective in your job and generally to make sure that things go smoothly.

Our standard projects are for anyone 16 and older to undertake valuable service work to benefit the communities in which we work. Almost all of our standard projects do not have any requirements and are suitable for anyone who wants to give back by volunteering with us. Available throughout the year, you can start anytime and choose your duration.
