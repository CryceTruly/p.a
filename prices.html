The price of each project includes

  1. accommodation
  2. food
  3. airport pick up and drop off
  4. necessary work-related transportation
  5. travel insurance
  6. medical insurance
  7. in-country induction with a staff member upon arrival
  8. pre-departure support from Volunteer Advisor by email and phone
  9. visa support (if applicable)


The price does not include:

  1. flights
  2. Visas

More about pricing

  1. Fees quoted in US Dollars.
  2. Projects lasting between listed durations are possible for the higher price level. For example, for a 22 day project the "4 weeks" price would apply.
  3. Any extensions made after the start of a project, if possible at all, will be charged at the "In-Country Extension" rate.
  4. Children ages 4-14 can be accommodated with parent or guardian at the "In-Country Extension" rate in some destinations.
  5. Families traveling with children ages 15+ each qualify for a 5% discount from their normal program fee. This is only for immediate family members and excludes cousins, boyfriends, girlfriends, and close friends.
  6. If you have volunteered with Pamoja Africa before, you’re eligible for

    7. A 10% discount when you sign up for another project with us at any time. This goes up to 15% if you sign up within three months of finishing your previous project.

    A 50% discount on the deposit to secure your spot on your next project.

    A 10% discount for each volunteer you recommend who signs up with us. This is cumulative, so that means that you could join a project for free (up to a duration of four weeks) if you sign up nine other volunteers. Your friend/s will also receive 5% off their project fee.

  7. Our current insurers can only provide coverage for volunteers up to age 65. Volunteers ages 66+ will need to obtain alternative travel and medical insurance.
  8. Some of our Projects for Professionals require extra registration costs and procedures before you arrive. Amounts range from US$50 to US$600. Please see the webpage for your project to see if extra registration fees apply.
